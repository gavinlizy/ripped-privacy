Ripped 隐私政策

最后更新:2026 年 6 月 18 日

Ripped 非常重视你的隐私。

  • 我们不收集任何个人数据。Ripped 没有账号系统、不要求注册、也不连接我们的任何服务器。
  • 你的训练数据只存储在你的设备上,我们无法访问。
  • Apple Health:仅在你主动开启"用 Apple Health 记录训练"开关后,Ripped 才会在你的设备本地与 Apple Health 交换数据(读取体重 / 体脂、写入训练记录)。这些数据由苹果管理,不会发送给我们。你可以随时在系统"健康"App 中撤销授权。
  • AI 教练:Ripped 可以把你的训练数据整理成一段文字。只有当你自己主动复制并粘贴到第三方 AI(如 Claude)时,这段内容才会离开你的设备;是否发送、发送给谁,完全由你决定。Ripped 本身不会自动上传。
  • 第三方:Ripped 不集成任何广告或数据分析 SDK。

Ripped Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Ripped takes your privacy seriously.

  • We do not collect any personal data. Ripped has no accounts, requires no sign-up, and connects to no servers of ours.
  • Your training data is stored only on your device, and we cannot access it.
  • Apple Health: Only when you turn on the "Track workouts with Apple Health" switch does Ripped exchange data locally with Apple Health (reading weight / body fat, writing workout records). This data is managed by Apple and is never sent to us. You can revoke access anytime in the Health app.
  • AI Coach: Ripped can compile your training data into text. It leaves your device only when you personally copy and paste it into a third-party AI (e.g. Claude) — you decide whether and where to send it. Ripped never uploads it automatically.
  • Third parties: Ripped contains no advertising or analytics SDKs.
