常见问题

  • 换了新手机,解锁的导出功能怎么找回?
    打开 设置,点「恢复购买」即可——一次性购买永久有效,不是订阅。
  • 「原生体能训练优先」是做什么的?
    开启后 Ripped 只记录组数、重量、次数,不写入 Apple Health、不记录心率和卡路里。适合用 Apple Watch 原生「体能训练」App 记录、并参加保险公司健身奖励计划的用户,避免重复或冲突。
  • 我的数据存在哪里?
    全部存在你自己的设备上。Ripped 没有账号、不联网,数据只在你主动导出时才离开手机。建议定期用「导出完整备份」存一份。

FAQ

  • I got a new phone — how do I restore my unlocked exports?
    Open Settings and tap "Restore purchase." It's a one-time purchase, yours forever — not a subscription.
  • What does "Defer to Apple Workout" do?
    When on, Ripped only logs sets, weight and reps — it won't write to Apple Health or record heart rate / calories. Ideal if you record with the native Apple Watch Workout app and take part in an insurer's fitness-rewards program, to avoid duplicate workouts.
  • Where is my data stored?
    Entirely on your device. Ripped has no account and no servers; your data only leaves the phone when you export it yourself. Use "Export full backup" to keep a copy.
